MY AKAN NAME

Description

This application enables users to discover their Akan names which are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Read more

Guide

Before you submit, ensure you've selected:

  • Your preferred name
  • Your gender
  • Day of birth
  • Month of birth
  • Year of birth
